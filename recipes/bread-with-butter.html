Bread With Butter

Description:

This simple recipe is a classic snack. Toasted bread with a smooth layer of butter, perfect for a quick bite or to complement a meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Slice of bread
  • Butter (as needed)

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread slice until golden and crispy.
  2. Spread butter evenly on the warm toast.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!

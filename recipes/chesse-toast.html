Cheese Toast

Description:

A simple delicous snack. Warm bread topped with a slice of melted cheese, perfect for a quick bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese (your choice, like cheddar or mozzarella)

Steps:

  1. Place the slice of chesse on top of the slice of bread.
  2. Toast in a skillet or sandwich maker until the chesse melts and the bread turns golden.
  3. Serve warm and enjoy!

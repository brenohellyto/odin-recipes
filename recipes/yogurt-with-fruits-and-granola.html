Yogurt with fruits and granola

Description:

A healty and refreshing snack. Creamy yogurt topped with fresh fruits and crunchy granola.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1/2 banana, sliced (or any fruit of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons of granola

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, add the yogurt
  2. Tip with banana slices
  3. Finish wit granola.
  4. Mix gently and enjoy!
